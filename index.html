All You Need To Know About EcoTargets!

World Description

Eco Targets is set in a desolate world, overrun by plants and wild life, bursting with life yet almost extinct of humanity. After a cataclysmic advancement in technology, AI's developed to seek the erasure of all humans, rendering cities into a shell of what they used to be.

Yet, humanity endured. In small cities and communities, they advanced what little basic technology was still available, and gathered ways to combat the invisible threat that could alter matter itself. In an uphill battle, the spirit of many never wavers, until the day the air itself isn't a threat to anyone's life.

Useful Terms

[ Hyper Seeds ] - AI's that can change the world around them and blend into reality itself.

[ Hyper World ] - An AI's domain, controlled completely by it.

[ Code Breakers ] - A sort of rudimentary AI, allowing users to change the system.

[ Chroma Goggles ] - A widely used set of goggles that allows the perception of Hyper Seeds, changing C-Rays into a perceivable image. One of the first defense methods regarding Hyper Seeds.

[ Runners ] - Mercenaries or fixers that work by commission, usually associated to corporations and weighed by rank.

[ J. Protocol ] - The Junkyard Protocol is a corporation known for scavenging and also owning affordable Runners with a wide range of jobs. They seemed to be funded by a wide array of backers, and are divided into different departments.
